# Definitions

## Clifford Number

The shortest evidenced path from a node to the Clifford Policy Machine.

## Clifford Policy Machine

The target node for the seed graph. It represents the institutional machine around Matt Clifford, the AI Opportunities Action Plan, the Prime Minister adviser role, public-sector AI adoption, national AI infrastructure, and state-market AI formation.

## Edge

A typed relationship between two nodes. An edge must have a claim, source IDs, evidence class, and status.

## Node

A person, company, policy object, government office, government unit, private forum, infrastructure policy, or abstract control-plane object.

## Receipt

The source material that supports an edge. A receipt can be an official page, public report, primary document, or local analysis note. Local analysis notes cannot replace public receipts for publication-critical claims.

## Listed

A source says the person or organization appears on a list or directory. This does not prove attendance.

## Registered

A source says the person or organization was registered for an event. This does not prove attendance.

## Attended

A source says the person or organization attended.

## Derived

The graph author made a structural inference from sourced facts. The inference must be marked and reversible.
