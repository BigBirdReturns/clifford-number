The Clifford Number

Seven degrees of UK AI state capture, with receipts.

Not conspiracy. Topology.

Pick a node.

The app returns the shortest evidenced path to the Clifford policy machine. Every hop has a claim, an evidence class, a status, and receipts.

What counts

A node can be a person, company, policy object, office, forum, government unit, or infrastructure layer. An edge is a typed public relationship. The graph does not score ideology, intent, character, private life, or guilt.

What does not count

Private contact data, intimate disclosures, raw registrant files, psychological claims, and per-person treatment of private attendees are excluded. Listed, registered, and attended are separate states.

The control question

Can every laugh survive a receipts click? If the answer is no, the edge does not ship.
