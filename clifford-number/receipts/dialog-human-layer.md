# Dialog human layer receipt note

This local note summarizes the public-role-only Dialog layer used in the seed graph.

The public claim is structural: Dialog is reported as a private invitation-only forum co-founded by Peter Thiel and Auren Hoffman. Public reporting says it convenes public officials, foreign government figures, and Silicon Valley executives at off-record retreats.

This repo does not reproduce raw registrant records, private contact data, intimate questionnaire answers, private predictions, or matchmaking fields. It preserves the distinction between listed, registered, and attended.

Seed edges from the Dialog layer are intentionally narrow:

```txt
person or company -> Dialog -> Matt Clifford -> Clifford Policy Machine
```

That means the app is mapping a public social adjacency layer, not making a claim of agreement, guilt, or attendance unless the edge status explicitly says attendance.
