# UK AI policy seed receipt note

This local note explains why the current seed graph uses the AI Opportunities Action Plan as the policy hinge.

The official GOV.UK announcement on 26 July 2024 appointed Matt Clifford to lead work on an AI Opportunities Action Plan and described an AI Opportunities Unit to help implement proposals. The official Action Plan was then published on 13 January 2025. It includes compute, data, talent, regulatory, public-sector adoption, procurement, interoperability, and UK Sovereign AI recommendations.

The graph target is not Matt Clifford as a private person. The graph target is the policy machine that forms around public appointment, policy authorship, state adoption, infrastructure, procurement, and public-private implementation.
